# Epic Portal App Summary

## Short Summary for Production Readiness

**Use this text in Epic's "App Summary" or "What does your app do?" field:**

---

MyChart FHIR App is a patient-facing mobile application that enables patients to securely access and view their health information from Epic Systems. The app provides patients with convenient access to their medical records, including patient demographics, laboratory results, vital signs, current medications, and appointment history. The application uses SMART on FHIR protocols with OAuth 2.0 authentication and PKCE for secure access, ensuring patient data privacy and security. The app supports both English and Arabic languages to serve diverse patient populations.

---
